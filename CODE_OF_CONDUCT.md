# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We commit to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or professional affiliation.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate with empathy, clarity, and professional respect.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Welcome questions and differing levels of experience.
- Give credit, protect confidential information, and disclose conflicts of interest.
- Accept maintainers' decisions and moderation in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, doxxing, and publication of private, customer, or employer information are not permitted.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository spaces and when representing the project in public or private community interactions.

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's preferred contact channel. Do not disclose sensitive details in a public issue. Maintainers will review reports promptly, preserve confidentiality where possible, and may warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove participants.

## Attribution

This policy is informed by the Contributor Covenant and established open-source community practices.
