# Governance

## Project Vision

Maintain an open, trustworthy, production-aware engineering handbook that supports AEM developers, senior engineers, technical leads, and future solution architects.

## Maintainers

Maintainers steward repository direction, releases, community health, and quality. The active owner map is maintained in [CODEOWNERS](.github/CODEOWNERS). New maintainers should demonstrate sustained, constructive contributions and sound technical and editorial judgment.

## Review Process

Every change requires automated checks and maintainer review. Changes affecting architecture, security, governance, or release policy require a relevant subject-matter reviewer. Authors do not approve their own substantial changes.

## Decision Process

Prefer documented consensus. Consequential or difficult-to-reverse decisions require an Architecture Decision Record. If consensus cannot be reached, maintainers decide after recording alternatives, evidence, and dissent.

## Contribution Levels

| Level            | Typical participation                          | Path forward                    |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Community member | Questions, discussions, and feedback           | Follow the code of conduct      |
| Contributor      | Issues, documentation, templates, and fixes    | Submit reviewed pull requests   |
| Reviewer         | Repeated domain or editorial review            | Demonstrate sustained expertise |
| Maintainer       | Governance, approval, releases, and moderation | Maintainer consensus            |

## Release Cycle

Releases are milestone-driven rather than date-driven. Patch releases may be issued when compatible corrections are ready. See [RELEASES.md](RELEASES.md).

## Community Principles

- Be respectful, inclusive, evidence-led, and transparent.
- Optimize for reader value and long-term maintenance.
- Protect customer, employer, and contributor confidentiality.
- Distinguish community guidance from official Adobe documentation.
- Preserve decision history and acknowledge contributors.
